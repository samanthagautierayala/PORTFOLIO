GINNY & GEORGIA AD CAMPAIGN

I designed an advertising campaign for the Netflix series Ginny & Georgia. The assignment required all the elements to be visually cohesive and unified, but each piece presenting new problems and opportunities unique to each medium. The following advertising presentation deck includes two posters, two billboards, apparel and merch items. This project was made as an assignment for the UM course 'STC202 - Advanced Graphic Design for Advertising.'
